## 0.1.0

- Initial release
- Multi-network support: Mainnet, Testnet, or custom RPC
- Type-safe RPC client with strongly typed responses
- Methods: `status`, `block`, `viewAccount`, `viewAccessKey`, `viewAccessKeyList`, `callFunction`, `validators`, `gasPrice`, `viewContractCode`
- Platform agnostic: works on iOS, Android, Web, Desktop
- Full integration tests against real NEAR networks
